About Us

We are David Torres and Jonathan García, two passionate software engineering students dedicated to making a difference. Despite our diverse hobbies and interests, we share a common goal: to protect the lives of infants with our project, Wannacry.

Our journey has been fueled by countless hours of hard work and collaboration. We believe that every child deserves a safe and secure environment, and with Wannacry, we aim to contribute to that vision.

Together, we are committed to creating a tool that not only raises awareness but also provides practical solutions to ensure the well-being of babies everywhere.

Thank you for joining us on this mission. Together, we can create a brighter and safer future for our children.
